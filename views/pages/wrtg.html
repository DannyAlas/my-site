First Ever Email Sent

As the Internet was not yet invented, this email was sent over the ARPANET.

ARPANET was the precursor to the internet - it was created by the Department of Defense to allow researchers to share information and resources. The network was initially limited to universities and research institutions. By 1983, ARPANET had over 4,000 connected computers and a growing number of e-mail users.
